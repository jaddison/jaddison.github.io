Your Company Culture is Broken

Thu 25 September 2014

As an employee, do you feel disillusioned or jaded in your job? Or, as a business founder or executive, does it seem like your team members are lacking synergy or unity?

There are few companies that get culture right. The reason is simple: most don’t consider culture important to the success of the company.

Ever heard this old adage?

A happy employee is a productive employee.

An employee’s happiness is directly related to their level of cultural comfort. Sure, when she first comes on board she’s excited to power ahead and achieve - but she’s likely also ignorant of the culture she’s entering into. A crappy culture will quickly sap the individual’s motivation. I’ve seen and heard it happen countless times. You probably have too.

It doesn’t have to be this way. This is an appeal to startups to make company culture the foundation of your business.
